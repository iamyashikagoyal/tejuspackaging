Who are we?

Read More >>

Welcome to Tejus Packaging, a unit of Tejus Polyfilms Pvt. Ltd., where tradition meets innovation in the world of industrial packaging solutions. For over 35 years, we have been at the forefront of manufacturing a diverse range of paper products and packaging materials, including LD Foam Sheets, LD Sheets, Synthetic Sheets, Non-Tearable Paper, Banner Sheets, and Tag Sheets. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has established us as the leading name in the packaging industry. At Tejus Packaging, we pride ourselves on our state-of-the-art RAJOO Bausano machinery, which stands as a testament to our dedication to technological advancement and efficiency. Our production capabilities are matched by an unwavering dedication to our certifications, evidenced by our extensive portfolio of over 150 satisfied customers from various industries such as FMCG, Pharmaceutical, Advertising, Offline Banner Advertisements, Tags, Books, and more. Our journey began with a vision to provide unparalleled packaging solutions that cater to the dynamic needs of businesses across sectors. Today, we continue to uphold this vision by delivering products that not only protect but also enhance the value of our certifications’ goods.

Our Commitment to Quality & Infrastructure

At Tejus Packaging, our infrastructure is equipped with the industry-leading Rajoo Bausano machinery, ensuring precision and excellence in every product we create. We adhere to a strict policy of using only globally certified polymers, which guarantees that our production standards meet international benchmarks. Our commitment to the environment is reflected in our materials, which are not only eco-friendly but also 100% recyclable, aligning with our sustainability goals. We take pride in our Food Grade certification, which underscores the safety and quality of our products. To deliver the finest quality, we employ advanced equipment and techniques designed to minimize gauge variation. This attention to detail ensures that our products consistently meet the high standards our clients expect from us. Furthermore, we offer versatility in our product range, with sheets that are Both Side Printable, catering to a myriad of applications and industries.
