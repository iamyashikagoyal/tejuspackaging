LD Sheets


Product Overview

LD Sheets for Tags: Crafted for excellence, our LD Sheets are the perfect foundation for high-quality tags. These sheets are made from premium Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE), ensuring a durable and flexible base for your tagging needs. With a smooth finish and uniform thickness, they provide an ideal surface for printing crisp and clear information. Whether for retail, logistics, or advertising, LD Sheets are designed to deliver long-lasting performance and versatility.

Key Features

  • ✓

    High Durability: Resistant to tearing and environmental stress, ensuring long-lasting tags.

  • ✓

    Printable Surface: Compatible with various printing technologies for sharp and vibrant prints.

  • ✓

    Versatile Use: Suitable for tags across industries, from retail to logistics.

  • ✓

    Engineered to provide consistent quality, ensuring your products are represented with reliability and style.

Common Applications

  • ▸

    Rice Tags: Strong, reliable sheets used widely in food and commodity packaging tags.

  • ▸

    Barcode Strips: Ensures accurate, durable printing for inventory and logistics tracking.

  • ▸

    Advertising Materials: Used in banners, posters, and other printed promotional items.
